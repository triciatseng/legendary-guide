fridgeTracker


So much food is wasted all over the world. In the US alone, 30-40% of the food supply is wasted, equaling more than 20 pounds of food per person per month.

At the consumer level, a lot of fresh groceries are not properly stored, or they are thrown out prematurely due to misunderstandings over sell/use by dates. And sometimes they're simply forgotten in the back of the fridge. Life happens.

Although this problem is definitely not one that can be solved overnight, the goal of fridgeTracker is to help families reduce their food waste using technology to educate and inspire.
